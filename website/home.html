What is NLP Institute Indonesia?

NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming) is a cutting-edge thinking technology that serves as the heart of communication and transformation across all aspects of human life, from business and leadership to health.

As a global innovation that has empowered millions, we introduce NLP Next Generation, moving beyond conventional techniques toward a systemic and generative approach.

Through 'field-based' techniques and a whole-system perspective (fourth position), this Third Generation NLP focuses on higher levels of learning including identity, vision, and mission to drive profound change for individuals, teams, and organizational culture as a whole.

Why We Are Different?

NLP Institute Indonesia (under PT. Inspirasi Cipta Indonesia) is a premier training and consulting firm established on October 30, 2007.

Founded by Irene Corry, the first and only authorized NLP Master Trainer in Indonesia affiliated with Robert Dilts and Judith DeLozier, we specialize in high-quality programs for Management, HRD, and International Certification.

We provide tailored Training, Coaching, and Assessment for corporate, government, and personal needs, dedicated to unlocking human potential through the advanced technology of Neuro-Linguistic Programming.
